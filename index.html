Verse Source:

Bhagavad Gita, Chapter XV, Verse 1

English Translation:

“They speak of an imperishable ashvattha tree with its roots above and branches below. Its leaves are the Vedic hymns, and one who knows this tree knows the Vedas.”

Transliteration:

ūrdhvamūlamadhaḥśākham aśvatthaṁ prāhur avyayam |

chhandāṁsi yasya parṇāni yastaṁ veda sa vedavit ||
